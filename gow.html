Assassin Creed II

Delve deeper into the secret society of assassins with the sequel to the blockbuster action-adventure game.

  • Game ID: BLES00669
  • Genre: Action, Stealth, Single
  • Rating 9.0

Assassin's Creed II invites you to step into the Renaissance-era Italy and embark on a mesmerizing journey of vengeance, conspiracy, and discovery. Play as Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a young nobleman turned Assassin, as he seeks to unravel the truth behind his family's betrayal. With a vast open-world environment featuring stunning cities like Florence, Venice, and Forlì, you'll engage in parkour-infused gameplay, scaling historic landmarks and seamlessly blending into crowds. Master the art of stealth and combat, utilizing an arsenal of weapons and tools to eliminate targets and manipulate your surroundings. Immerse yourself in a captivating narrative woven into the rich tapestry of history, as you navigate political intrigue and unlock the mysteries of the ancient Assassin Order. Assassin's Creed II is not just a game; it's an immersive historical adventure that will leave an indelible mark on your gaming journey.
